What is NEW in FC Mobile 26?

Anniversary celebrations are here! Happy Birthday, EA Sports FC Mobile!

This year’s 2nd Anniversary Event theme is “The Club Is Yours”. Players get to help shape the event by voting on kits, boots, ball designs, and which iconic players will appear. There will also be milestone rewards, gallery shards for special anniversary players, and DIY star player mechanics allowing fans to customize their own event icons.

Anniversary Event and Ballon d’Or

The new Anniversary version brings changes to the game engine. First, the KICK-OFF-RUSH that troubled many players has been fixed. Thanks to the technical team, the lofted through ball system is now more realistic, preventing low-rated players from gaining an unfair early advantage and promoting fairer gameplay. Second, referee decisions have been improved in the new version, reducing incorrect calls caused by simple physical contact that previously interrupted team attacks.

The Ballon d’Or Event in FC Mobile honors this incredible achievement with themed rewards and limited-time cards. Players can take part in special matches, unlock exclusive Dembélé items, and relive the journey that led him to football’s most prestigious individual award. Celebrate greatness and add the Ballon d’Or winner to your ultimate team!

Congratulations to Ousmane Dembélé on winning the 2025 Ballon d’Or!
