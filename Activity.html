EA Sports FC Mobile Anniversary Event Full Guide

The long wait is over, and with the massive FC Mobile 26 update now live, the Anniversary Event has officially begun. Running for four weeks (28 days), this event is packed with chapters, milestones, featured players, and plenty of rewards. Here’s a complete breakdown of everything available.

The event features five main chapters - Info, Main, Rewards 1, Gallery, and Team Badge. The Info chapter provides an overview of the event, including a special “DIY Your Star Player” section. Each week, players can claim one card or card art, and by the end, one of these selections will be finalized. Alongside this, there are 11 featured players to collect across different reward chapters. These aren’t playable cards but collectible images, and collecting all of them is required to unlock a special 110 OVR card at the end of the event. Currently, in Rewards 1, the three featured collectibles are Van Dijk, Amad Diallo, and Doue. As the weeks progress, Rewards 2, 3, and 4 will unlock, offering more collectibles until all 11 are available.

The Main chapter revolves around training rewards. By completing daily skill games or matches, players earn 200 Anniversary Tokens. These tokens unlock milestone rewards that require a set number of training completions. Milestones include 500 gems (1 reward), 50 rank-up points (3 rewards), 5 million coins (7 rewards), a 104-109 OVR Anniversary player pack (10 rewards), and 150 rank-up points (15 rewards). Since one training reward can be completed per day, it will take 15 days to clear all milestones. Additional tokens can be obtained by watching ads (60 tokens), purchasing with FC Points, or through a special pack costing 200 Anniversary Tokens. This pack unlocks on 16 October and can be opened up to 999 times, offering rewards like shards, coins, gems, transfer tokens, and rank-up points.

The Rewards 1 chapter allows tokens to be spent on different items, requiring a total of 1,250 Anniversary Tokens to clear. Rewards include shards, coins, gems, and packs. More reward chapters will follow in the coming weeks.

The Gallery chapter introduces a rotating set of players each week, claimable by spending shards. Currently, Week 1’s lineup is available, with new cards coming every week until the event ends.Beyond the main event structure, new exchanges, special store offers, and fresh Extra Time offers have been introduced, providing even more ways to maximize progress.

Overall, the Anniversary Event is one of the most feature-packed celebrations in FC Mobile’s history, rewarding consistent participation with everything from free Icons to premium packs and milestone rewards. With four full weeks of content, players have plenty to look forward to as the event unfolds.
